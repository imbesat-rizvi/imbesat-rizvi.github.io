About

Md. Imbesat Hassan Rizvi - CV, Scholar, LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter

I am a Data Scientist at Hewlett-Packard (HP Inc.), Bangalore, India. In my current role at the organization, I am involved in building deep learning-based solutions for image search and recommendations. In the past, I have also worked in collaboration on NLP-based projects for identifying relatively important customer issues from product ratings using attention-based LSTM network for issue identification.

I am also currently pursuing an online course on Deep Learning offered through Tokyo Data Science (TokyoDS) which is being taught by Prof. Michal Fabinger.

I have completed my Master’s from the Department of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS), Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. At CDS, I have worked on graph theoretic-based combinatorial generation of molecular structures for drug discovery starting from a lead compound, which culminated into a book chapter and a research paper publication.

Prior to IISc, I have studied Mechanical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, IIT (ISM), Dhanbad and have worked at Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) for about two years.

And of course, at last, I am a novice Urdu poet, a huge Harry Potter and The Big Bang Theory fan.
